About Us

  • Overview

    During the tumultuous period of World War II, BOSE emerged as a bastion of resilience. It became a crucible of skill and expertise, training a legion of technicians. Such was its importance that even Lord Wavell, the Viceroy of India at the time, made fervent visits. From its humble origins, with an initial intake capacity of a mere forty students in the Diploma in Civil Engineering program, BOSE has burgeoned into a sprawling institution of learning. Today, it offers diplomas in a diverse array of engineering disciplines, including Civil, Mechanical, Electrical, Electronics and Telecommunication, and many others, totaling eleven disciplines in all. With a cumulative intake capacity of 738 students annually, BOSE has evolved into a bustling hive of intellectual activity, boasting a student body exceeding 2200. Noteworthy advancements mark BOSE’s journey into the contemporary era. The institution has garnered accolades such as accreditation from the National Board of Accreditation (NBA) in three disciplines, a testament to its unwavering commitment to excellence. Furthermore, strategic partnerships with industry giants like FESTO, Schneider Electric, and Philips Education underscore BOSE’s forward-looking approach, aimed at fostering innovation and practical learning experiences. The graduates of BOSE, true to their alma mater’s ethos, have carved illustrious paths in various domains. They adorn academia, occupying esteemed positions in institutions like the IITs, NITs, and other premier engineering colleges. Moreover, they lend their expertise to the public and private sectors, serving in organizations such as the Railways, SAIL, L&T, Infosys, and a myriad of others. Noteworthy are their triumphs in skill competitions, where they have consistently showcased their prowess and brought laurels to the institution. Despite its illustrious past and present achievements, BOSE’s journey has not been devoid of challenges. Efforts to elevate it to the status of a degree-granting institution have been made in the past, albeit with elusive success. Yet, undeterred by setbacks, BOSE remains resolute in its pursuit of excellence, guided by the enduring legacy of its founders and the indomitable spirit of its students and faculty alike.

  • Our Mission

    1. To nurture the development of skill, attitude and core competence; inspire innovation and promote distinction in students to enable them to stand out in their academic pursuits.
    2. To hone the raw talent in the students and produce intellectually sound, technically resourceful and professionally competent, diploma graduates to stride into the challenging world to make difference.

  • Our Vision

    To accomplish and sustain academic eminence in the sphere of technical education in the state of Odisha and become an acclaimed and the most sought after Diploma Institution, every young and aspiring student would feel proud to be a part of.

Our Services
